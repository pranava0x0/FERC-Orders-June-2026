Monza Tech operates Sentinel Grid, a grid reliability prediction platform. We score daily failure probability across 6 US RTOs using EIA demand, weather, and LMP congestion data. Our walk-forward validated model detects 87% of    reliability events with a 13% false positive rate. As FERC considers large load interconnection rules, we want to highlight that forward-looking grid stress prediction, using publicly available data, is technically viable today and could inform interconnection study processes.
